About Boltron Integrated Farms

Boltron Integrated Farms is a multi-activity farming initiative designed to create a balanced and self-supporting agricultural ecosystem.

Instead of depending on one farming activity alone, we integrate multiple sectors of farming so they support each other naturally and economically.

Our vision is to build a model farm that combines food production, animal rearing, natural input generation, waste-to-value systems, rural business opportunity, and sustainable agricultural growth.

Our Integrated Farming Model

Our farm is designed around interconnected systems where one activity supports another, improving resource efficiency, soil health, sustainability, and income diversity.

Livestock & Poultry

Integrated turkey, country chicken, broiler, duck, quail and goat farming to create multiple income sources and natural manure cycles.

Crops & Cultivation

Vegetable, fruit and mushroom cultivation supported by organic inputs and soil-health focused practices within the farm ecosystem.

Natural & Support Systems

Bee keeping, azolla cultivation, vermicompost production, solar and waste utilization to recycle resources and reduce input costs.
